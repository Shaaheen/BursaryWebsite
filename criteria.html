Criteria

Eligible applicants will have to meet these basic requirements as listed below: must be from the Western Cape.  must have a valid South African Identity Document.  must apply to study at the University of Cape Town as applications to other institutions of higher education will not be considered.  must be in need of financial assistance.  must show a strong academic record (November Grade 11, June and September Grade 12 academic results provided).  Candidates from a previously disadvantaged background will be given preference  Great leadership skills will be advantageous. All selected candidates must be motivated, committed, eager to improve their living conditions, having a burning passion for learning and would like to assist in the development of their community.
